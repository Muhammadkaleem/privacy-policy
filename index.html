Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 5, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to QR Loom, developed by Dev Bytes. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal or sensitive information from our users. QR Loom accesses your device's camera and gallery permissions to scan QR codes and save generated QR codes in your gallery.

Permissions We Use

  • Camera: To scan QR codes.
  • Read Media Images: To save generated QR codes to your device's gallery.

Third-Party Services

Currently, QR Loom does not use any third-party services such as advertisements or analytics. However, we may implement ads in future versions, and this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data Retention

We do not collect or retain any user data. All QR codes generated are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us or third parties.

Security

As we do not collect or store user data, no security or encryption measures are implemented in the app.

Children's Privacy

QR Loom is suitable for users of all ages. We do not collect any information from children or users in general.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
